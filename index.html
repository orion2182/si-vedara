Character Profile

Chisa Kuchiba

Appearance

Chisa is a pale young woman with long, glossy black hair ending near her waist and narrow pale red eyes with white pupils and a beauty mark under the corner of her right eye.

Her Tacet Mark is located on her right upper arm.

Clothing

Chisa sports a black Japanese sailor uniform with a red neck scarf and white collar; an old Startorch Academy uniform that she still chooses to wear, likely out of habit. Over top she wears a loose-fitting long-sleeved black jacket that fades into a light grey. She wears a pair of shiny black and heeled penny loafer shoes as well as dark socks going halfway up her calves.

Her hair is festooned by a thin red ribbon tied at her right temple. On her neck, she wears a black collar-type Resonator suppression device due to her unstable Forte control.

Resonance Power

Eye of Unraveling - Testing indicates Chisa possesses an extraordinary cognitive ability to intuitively discern the structure, form, and movement of observed objects. Notes

Subject recounts perceiving "threads" that cause objects to unravel. Preliminary analysis suggests these "threads" are not actual entities but visual manifestations exclusive to her vision.
